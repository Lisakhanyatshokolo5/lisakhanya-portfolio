About Me

Aspiring Software Engineer

My passion for technology started in high school, where Information Technology sparked my curiosity about how software is built. That interest led me to complete a software development learnership at CAPACITI, where I gained practical experience solving real-world problems and learned the importance of building functional, user-focused solutions.

After the learnership, I worked in a marketing role where I found myself naturally gravitating toward the technical side of the business—updating websites, improving content, and optimizing SEO. It became clear that software development was where my passion truly belonged, which inspired me to pursue a Diploma in ICT Application Development at the Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT).

Today, I enjoy building full-stack applications using Java, Spring Boot, React, Laravel, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, and MySQL. I particularly enjoy designing clean, maintainable code, developing REST APIs, and creating responsive user interfaces that provide a great user experience.

I'm always looking for opportunities to learn new technologies, improve my problem-solving skills, and collaborate with others. My goal is to grow into a software engineer who builds reliable, scalable applications while continuously learning from experienced developers and contributing to meaningful projects.
